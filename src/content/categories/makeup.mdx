## How to Prepare for Your Makeup Appointment

The secret to flawless, long-lasting makeup starts with how you prep your skin before you even sit in the makeup chair.

### The Week Before Your Event
- **Focus on Hydration:** Drink plenty of water and use a good moisturizer daily. Makeup sits beautifully on plump, hydrated skin but will cling to dry, flaky patches.
- **Do Not Try New Products:** Avoid introducing any new, harsh skincare products (like strong retinol or new chemical peels) the week of your event to prevent unexpected breakouts or redness.
- **Gently Exfoliate:** The night before your appointment, gently exfoliate your face and lips to create a smooth canvas.

### On the Day of Your Appointment
- **Come with a Clean Face:** Arrive at your appointment with a completely clean face, free of any leftover mascara or foundation.
- **Moisturize:** You can apply your normal, lightweight daily moisturizer, but skip heavy oils or SPF, as these can cause "flashback" in professional photography.
- **Wear a Button-Down Shirt:** Wear a top that you do not have to pull over your head later, so you do not smudge your fresh makeup when you get dressed for your event.
