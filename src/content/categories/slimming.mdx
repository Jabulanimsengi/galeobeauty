## What to Expect During and After Slimming Treatments

Slimming injections and fat-dissolving treatments require a healthy lifestyle to support the results.

### Immediately After Treatment
- **Mild Swelling:** It is normal to experience temporary swelling, tenderness, and sometimes slight bruising in the areas where the injections were administered. This usually subsides within a few days.
- **Hydration is Key:** Just like fat freezing, the destroyed fat cells are processed through your lymphatic system. You must drink plenty of water (at least 2-3 liters a day) to flush the fat from your body.

### Supporting Your Results
- **Maintain a Healthy Diet:** These treatments are not a substitute for a healthy lifestyle. To see the best results and maintain them long-term, you must combine the treatments with a balanced diet and regular physical activity.
- **Avoid Heavy Exercise for 24 Hours:** Give your body a short rest immediately after the injections, but after 24 hours, light to moderate exercise can actually help stimulate your lymphatic system and improve results.
