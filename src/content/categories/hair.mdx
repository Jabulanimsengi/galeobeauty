## How to Care for Your Hair After Your Appointment

The way you treat your hair at home makes a massive difference in how long your colour lasts and how healthy your hair feels.

### Caring for Fresh Colour
- **Wait to Wash:** Try to wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair to allow the colour to fully set into the cuticle.
- **Use Professional Products:** Always use a sulphate-free, colour-safe shampoo and conditioner. Drugstore products can strip your new colour almost immediately.
- **Cool Down:** Wash your hair with lukewarm water instead of hot water, as heat opens the hair cuticle and lets colour fade faster.

### Caring for Smoothing Treatments (Brazilian/Keratin)
- **The First 72 Hours:** Depending on the specific treatment we used, you may need to avoid washing, tying up, or clipping your hair for up to 3 days to prevent dents. We will confirm this with you before you leave.
- **Strictly Sulphate-Free:** Sulphates will break down the keratin treatment prematurely. Only use the recommended aftercare range to get the full lifespan out of your smoothing treatment.
