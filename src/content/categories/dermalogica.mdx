## How to Care for Your Skin After a Dermalogica Treatment

When you leave the salon, your skin barrier is open and actively absorbing. Depending on whether you had a basic facial, a chemical peel, or microneedling, your aftercare is crucial for best results.

### The First 24 Hours
- **Do Not Touch:** Keep your hands off your face to avoid introducing bacteria to freshly cleared pores.
- **Skip the Gym:** Avoid intense exercise, saunas, and steam rooms. Sweat can irritate freshly exfoliated skin and cause breakouts.
- **No Makeup:** Try to avoid applying makeup for the rest of the day to let your skin breathe and absorb the professional products.

### Caring for Peels and Microneedling
- **Expect Peeling:** If you had a Pro Power Peel, you may experience light flaking or peeling on days 3 to 5. Do not pick or pull at the skin!
- **Pause Active Ingredients:** Stop using your active serums at home (Vitamin C, Retinol, AHA/BHA scrubs) for at least 3 to 5 days, or as advised by your therapist.
- **Sun Protection is Non-Negotiable:** Your skin will be highly sensitive to UV damage. Apply a broad-spectrum SPF 50 every single morning and reapply if you are outdoors.
