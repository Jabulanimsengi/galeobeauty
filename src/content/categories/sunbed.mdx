## How to Get the Most Out of Your Spray Tan

A flawless spray tan is 50% professional application and 50% how well you prep and care for your skin at home.

### Before Your Spray Tan
- **Exfoliate and Shave:** Shave and thoroughly exfoliate your entire body 24 hours *before* your appointment. Do not do this on the day of your tan, as it can leave your pores open and create a "speckled" look.
- **Skip the Lotions:** Arrive with completely clean, dry skin. Do not apply moisturizers, deodorants, perfumes, or makeup, as these create a barrier that stops the tan from developing evenly.
- **Wear Loose Clothing:** Bring dark, loose-fitting clothing to wear home. Tight clothes, jeans, or bra straps will rub the tan off before it has a chance to develop.

### After Your Spray Tan
- **Stay Dry while it Develops:** Do not shower, sweat, or get caught in the rain for the recommended development time (usually 8 to 12 hours).
- **The First Rinse:** When you take your first shower, the water will run brown—this is just the cosmetic bronzer washing away, not your actual tan! Rinse with lukewarm water until it runs clear, and avoid harsh scrubbing.
- **Moisturize Daily:** Once your tan has developed, keeping your skin heavily moisturized is the key to making it last. A well-hydrated tan fades evenly, while dry skin causes the tan to look patchy and crack.
