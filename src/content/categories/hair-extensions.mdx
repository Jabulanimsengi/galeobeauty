## How to Care for Your Hair Extensions

Premium human hair extensions are an investment. Because they are not attached to your scalp, they do not receive your natural nourishing oils, meaning your aftercare routine is vital to keeping them soft, shiny, and tangle-free.

### Daily Maintenance
- **Brush Gently and Often:** Brush your hair 2 to 3 times a day using a specialized extension brush (loop brush or boar bristle). Always hold the hair at the root to prevent pulling on the bonds, and brush from the ends, slowly working your way up.
- **Sleep Safely:** Never go to bed with wet extensions, as this causes severe matting. Before sleeping, braid your hair loosely or tie it in a low ponytail and use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction.

### Washing and Styling
- **Sulphate-Free is Mandatory:** Only use professional, sulphate-free, and alcohol-free shampoos and conditioners. Sulphates strip the hair and break down the adhesive bonds in tape-ins.
- **Condition the Ends Only:** Apply conditioner only to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair. Never apply conditioner or oil near the roots or bonds, as this will cause the extensions to slip out.
- **Use Heat Protection:** Because they are real human hair, you can blow-dry, curl, and straighten your extensions. However, you must always use a high-quality thermal protectant spray before applying any heat.
