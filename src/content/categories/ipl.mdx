## How to Care for Your Skin Before and After IPL

IPL requires a commitment to a course of treatments and careful attention to pre and post-care to ensure safe and effective results.

### Before Your IPL Session
- **Shave, Do Not Wax:** You must shave the treatment area 12 to 24 hours before your appointment. Do not wax, epilate, or pluck for at least 4 weeks prior, as the IPL needs the hair root intact under the skin to target it.
- **Avoid Sun Exposure:** Your skin must not be tanned (real or fake) before treatment. Avoid direct sun exposure, sunbeds, and self-tanners for at least 2 weeks before your session to prevent burns and pigmentation.
- **Skip the Actives:** Avoid using Retinol, AHAs, BHAs, or any exfoliating acids on the treatment area for a few days before your session.

### After Your IPL Session
- **Cool It Down:** It is normal for the area to feel slightly warm or look mildly pink, like a very mild sunburn, for a few hours. A cool compress or aloe vera gel can help soothe it.
- **Strict Sun Protection:** Your skin will be extremely sensitive to UV light. Keep the treated area out of direct sunlight and wear SPF 50 religiously to prevent dark spots (hyperpigmentation).
- **No Heat:** Avoid hot baths, saunas, steam rooms, and intense exercise for 24 to 48 hours after treatment.
- **Let the Hair Fall Out naturally:** Over the next 1 to 3 weeks, the treated hairs will slowly push their way out of the follicle and shed. Do not pluck them! You can gently exfoliate the area after a week to help them along.
