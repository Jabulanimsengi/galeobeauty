## What to Expect After Fat Freezing

Fat freezing requires no downtime, but understanding how your body processes the treatment will help you achieve the best results.

### The Immediate Aftermath
- **Numbness and Redness:** The treated area will feel extremely cold, firm, and numb immediately after the applicator is removed. Your therapist will massage the frozen tissue to break down the fat cells, which can feel strange but is crucial for results.
- **Mild Swelling or Bruising:** It is completely normal to experience some swelling, tenderness, or mild bruising in the treated area for a few days to a couple of weeks following the session.
- **Normal Activities:** You can return to your normal daily activities, including exercise, immediately after your appointment.

### Getting the Best Results
- **Drink Plenty of Water:** Your lymphatic system is responsible for flushing out the destroyed fat cells. Drink at least 2 to 3 liters of water a day to support this process.
- **Be Patient:** Fat freezing is not an overnight fix. You will start to see initial changes around 3 to 4 weeks, but the most dramatic results will appear between 8 and 12 weeks as your body continues to clear the fat cells.
