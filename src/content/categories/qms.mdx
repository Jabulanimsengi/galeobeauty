## How to Care for Your Skin After a QMS Facial

A QMS facial leaves your skin deeply infused with premium collagen and hyaluronic acid. To maintain that incredible post-facial bounce and glow, follow these simple aftercare steps.

### Protecting the Glow
- **Leave the Products On:** Try not to wash your face on the evening of your facial. Let the active collagen serums and rich moisturizers continue to work their magic overnight.
- **Avoid Heavy Makeup:** Give your skin a chance to breathe. If you must wear makeup, try to stick to a light mineral powder or a tinted SPF rather than a heavy, pore-blocking liquid foundation.
- **Stay Hydrated:** Drink plenty of water. The hyaluronic acid applied during your facial works by drawing moisture into the skin; drinking water helps maximize this plumping effect.

### Long-Term Maintenance
- **Stick to the Routine:** QMS results compound over time. For the best anti-aging results, we recommend using the QMS Day and Night Collagen serums at home between your salon visits.
- **Sun Protection:** Always protect your newly refined skin with a high-quality SPF 50 every day to prevent UV rays from breaking down your skin's natural collagen.
