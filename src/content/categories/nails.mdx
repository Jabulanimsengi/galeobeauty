## How to Care for Your Nails Between Appointments

To keep your nails looking perfect and to protect your natural nail bed, follow these simple aftercare steps.

### Daily Maintenance
- **Use Cuticle Oil:** Apply a good quality cuticle oil at least once a day. This keeps the skin around your nails soft and prevents the gel or acrylic from becoming brittle and lifting.
- **Wear Gloves:** Whenever you are doing dishes, cleaning with harsh chemicals, or gardening, put on a pair of gloves. Water and chemicals are the biggest enemies of nail enhancements.
- **Your Nails Are Not Tools:** Never use your nails to open cans, pick off stickers, or pry things apart. This will cause the enhancement to chip or, worse, tear your natural nail.

### When It Is Time for a Fill
- **Do Not Pick or Peel:** If you notice a chip or lifting, do not pull at it. Peeling off gel or acrylic rips off the top layers of your natural nail, making them weak and paper-thin.
- **Book Regularly:** To prevent lifting and structural imbalance (which causes painful breaks), we recommend booking your fill every 2 to 3 weeks depending on how fast your nails grow.
