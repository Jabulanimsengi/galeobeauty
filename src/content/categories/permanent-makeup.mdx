## How to Care for Your Permanent Makeup

The healing process is just as important as the procedure itself when it comes to permanent makeup. Your results will heavily depend on how strictly you follow the aftercare.

### The First 10 Days
- **Keep It Dry:** Do not get the treated area wet. Avoid swimming, heavy sweating, saunas, and direct water pressure in the shower.
- **Do Not Pick or Scratch:** As the area heals, it will lightly scab and flake. It will feel itchy, but you absolutely must not pick or pull at the flakes, or you will pull the pigment right out of your skin, leaving patchy results.
- **Apply the Healing Ointment:** Use only the specific healing balm provided by your technician. Apply a very thin, barely-there layer as instructed—do not smother the area, as it needs to breathe to heal.
- **No Makeup or Skincare on the Area:** Keep all foundations, powders, and active skincare products away from the healing tattoo.

### Long-Term Maintenance
- **Protect It from the Sun:** UV rays are the biggest enemy of permanent makeup. Once fully healed, always apply a high SPF over your brows and lips to prevent the pigment from fading prematurely or changing colour.
- **Book Your Touch-Up:** Permanent makeup is a two-step process. You will need a perfecting touch-up session 4 to 6 weeks after your initial appointment to fill in any areas that faded during healing.
