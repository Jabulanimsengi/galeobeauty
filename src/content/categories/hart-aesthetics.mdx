## How to Care for Your Skin After Injectables

After an injectable treatment, proper aftercare is essential to ensure the product settles perfectly and to minimize any risk of bruising or swelling.

### The First 24 Hours
- **Stay Upright:** Do not lie down flat for at least 4 hours after anti-wrinkle injections to prevent the product from migrating.
- **No Rubbing:** Do not massage or rub the treated areas for at least 24 hours.
- **Avoid Heat and Exertion:** Skip the gym, saunas, hot tubs, and direct sun exposure for 24 to 48 hours to minimize swelling.
- **Skip the Alcohol:** Avoid drinking alcohol for 24 hours after your treatment, as it can thin the blood and increase the likelihood of bruising.

### Managing Swelling and Bruising
- **Cool Compresses:** If you experience slight swelling (especially common after lip fillers), you can apply a cold compress gently to the area for short periods.
- **Be Patient:** It takes up to 14 days for anti-wrinkle injections to fully take effect, and up to 2 weeks for dermal fillers to completely settle and integrate with your tissue.
