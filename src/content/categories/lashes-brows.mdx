## How to Care for Your Lashes and Brows

Good aftercare is the secret to making your lash extensions last and keeping your brow lifts looking fresh.

### Caring for Lash Extensions
- **The First 24 Hours:** Keep your lashes completely dry. Avoid showers, steam, sweat, and tears so the lash adhesive can fully cure.
- **Clean Them Daily:** Wash your lashes every single day with a lash-safe foaming cleanser and a soft brush. Build-up of natural oils and makeup will break down the glue and cause premature shedding.
- **Brush Them:** Gently brush your lashes morning and night with a clean spoolie wand to keep them fluffy and untangled.
- **Sleep Carefully:** Try to sleep on your back. Sleeping face-down into your pillow will crush the extensions and cause them to fall out on the outer corners.
- **No Mascara or Oil:** Never use mascara or an eyelash curler on extensions. Avoid all oil-based skincare and makeup removers around your eyes, as oil dissolves the adhesive.

### Caring for Lash Lifts and Brow Lamination
- **Keep it Dry:** Just like extensions, keep the area completely dry and free of steam for the first 24 hours.
- **Condition:** A few days after your lift or lamination, start applying a nourishing brow or lash serum daily. The lifting process can be slightly drying, so hydration keeps the hairs healthy and glossy.
