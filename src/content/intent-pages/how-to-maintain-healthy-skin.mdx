## Understanding How To Maintain Healthy Skin

Healthy skin is defined by "Homeostasis"—a state where the skin's moisture levels, oil production, and bacterial microbiome are in perfect balance. Biologically, the skin's primary enemy is **Oxidative Stress**. This is the microscopic damage caused by UV rays, pollution, and blue light from screens, which physically "chops" your collagen fibers into pieces.

To maintain [Healthy Skin](/prices/dermalogica), you must focus on **Antioxidant Protection**. It is not enough to just "hydrate"; you must protect the cellular DNA from the environmental toxins that trigger premature aging and inflammation.

## Our Professional Advice

At Galeo Beauty, we advocate for a clinical-grade "Daily Defense" strategy.

### 1. The Vitamin C Shield
Every morning, without exception, you must apply a stabilized **Vitamin C serum**. Vitamin C is a powerful electron donor; it "sacrifices" itself to neutralize free radicals from the sun before they can damage your skin cells. It is your primary biological insurance policy against dullness and dark spots.

### 2. Transepidermal Water Loss (TEWL) Prevention
Most people have "unhealthy" skin simply because they allow their moisture to evaporate. You must use a moisturizer rich in **Squalane or Hyaluronic Acid**. These molecules act as a microscopic "lid," sealing the moisture inside the epidermis and ensuring your skin cells stay plump and functional.

### 3. Professional Skin Mapping
The skin is dynamic; it changes with the seasons, your hormones, and your age. To maintain health, you must have a [Professional Skin Mapping](/prices/dermalogica/pro-skin-60) session every 3 months. This allows us to adjust your chemical routine before a small concern (like slight dehydration) turns into a major clinical issue (like a broken barrier).
