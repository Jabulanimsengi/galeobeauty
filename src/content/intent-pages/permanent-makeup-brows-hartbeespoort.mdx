## Why brows change the whole face

Brows frame the eyes and affect how polished the whole face looks. Even a subtle improvement in brow shape or fullness can make mornings feel much easier and your features more balanced.

## Microblading vs powder vs hybrid brows

Hair strokes tend to suit clients who want a finer, more natural look, while powder and hybrid approaches suit clients who want more density, softness or definition through the body of the brow.

## What makes permanent brows look natural

The most flattering result usually comes from choosing the right technique for your skin and keeping the overall shape soft enough to enhance your features rather than overpower them.
