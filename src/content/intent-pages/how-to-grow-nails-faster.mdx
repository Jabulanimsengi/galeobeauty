## Understanding How To Grow Nails Faster

Biologically, you cannot "speed up" the mitosis (cell division) in your nail matrix beyond your genetic limit. Your fingernails naturally grow at a rate of approximately **3mm per month**. However, most people feel their nails "don't grow" because the nails are breaking at the tip faster than they can grow from the base.

To [Grow Nails Faster](/services/nails), your objective is not to stimulate the root, but to **Protect the Length**. If you can prevent the tip from splitting, peeling, or snapping for 90 days, you will suddenly have the long, natural nails you've always desired.

## Our Professional Advice

Achieving extreme natural nail length requires a focus on metabolic health and mechanical surface protection.

### 1. Stimulating the Matrix (Blood Flow)
The "Matrix" is the living part of the nail tucked under your cuticle. To maximize growth potential, you must increase blood flow to this area. Gently massaging your cuticles daily with a nutrient-rich oil (like Jojoba or Vitamin E) increases oxygen delivery to the matrix, ensuring that the new cells being produced are thick, strong, and well-formed.

### 2. The Structured Overlay Shield
If your natural nails are too thin to support their own weight, they will inevitably break once they reach the 5mm mark. We recommend a [Structured Gel or BIAB Overlay](/services/nails). This acts as a "plastic cast," physically reinforcing the nail and preventing it from bending and breaking. This is the "safe zone" that allows your nails to grow to extreme lengths without environmental trauma.

### 3. Internal Keratin Building Blocks
Nail plates require Biotin, Iron, and Zinc to form a dense keratin matrix. If you are deficient in these nutrients, your nails will be biologically "mushy" even with external care. Maintaining a diet rich in these minerals ensures that the "raw material" your body uses to manufacture your nails is of the highest possible quality.
