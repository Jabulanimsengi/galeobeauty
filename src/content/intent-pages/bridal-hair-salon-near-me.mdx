## Why a Bridal Trial is Mandatory

We never guess on your wedding morning. The trial is when we experiment. We figure out exactly how your hair holds a curl, where the veil should anchor, and how the style complements your dress neckline.

## Adding Dream Volume

Most of those massive, romantic boho braids you see online involve extensions. We specialize in both permanent (Genius Weave) and temporary (Clip-in) extensions to give you the massive volume required for editorial bridal looks.

## Accommodating the Bridal Party

We excel at logistics. We can host your entire bridal party in our luxurious estate salon, providing coffee and a calm environment, or we can travel directly to your wedding venue in the Harties area.
