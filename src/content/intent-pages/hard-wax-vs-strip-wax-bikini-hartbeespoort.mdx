## How Soft (Strip) Wax Works

Soft wax is spread thinly, a cloth strip is pressed over it, and it is ripped off. It is incredibly fast and efficient for removing fine hair over large zones like the legs. However, it sticks strongly to the skin, meaning it cannot be applied over the same area twice without risking tearing or bruising.

## How Hard (Hot) Wax Works

Hard wax is applied thicker and allowed to cool. As it cools, it 'shrink-wraps' around the hair, completely ignoring the skin beneath. The therapist then peels the wax itself off. It is the absolute gold standard for Brazilian, Hollywood, and facial waxing.

## The Pain Difference

Because Hard Wax only grips the hair and not the skin, it is significantly less painful for intimate areas. It also allows the therapist to safely go over a stubborn patch a second time without worrying about skin lifts or burns.
