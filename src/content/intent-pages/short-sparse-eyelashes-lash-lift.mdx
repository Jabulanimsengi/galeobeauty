## The Illusion of Length

Most clients who complain of "short lashes" actually have a decent natural length that is vertically repressed. If your lashes point downward or out horizontally, they are invisible from a front-facing angle. By chemically lifting the lashes from the absolute root and setting them into a 90-degree flare, we reveal the hidden length that was already there. 

Furthermore, the tips of human eyelashes are notoriously translucent due to UV exposure. This "invisible tip" can account for up to 30% of your lash length. By applying a [Permanent Tint](/prices/lashes-and-brows), we bring the color all the way to the tip, making your lashes look instantly longer without adding a single millimetre of synthetic hair.

## Ditching the Mascara

The modern [Lash Lift & Tint](/prices/lashes-and-brows) provides a clean, "woke up like this" aesthetic that mascara cannot replicate. Mascara is heavy; its weight often causes natural lashes to droop throughout the day. A lash lift is a structural change within the hair itself, meaning your lashes stay perfectly curled even after a shower, a gym session, or a swim in the ocean.

## Incredible Low Maintenance

High-performance beauty shouldn't require high-performance upkeep. Unlike extensions, which require careful cleaning and "brushing," a Lash Lift only requires you to wait 24 hours for the keratin bonds to harden. After that window, your routine remains unchanged. This is the perfect service for the busy professional or athlete who wants to look refined without the "fussy" maintenance of traditional lash extensions.
