## Why regular peels and serums are not enough for melasma

Melasma is driven by deep melanocyte activity that surface-level brightening cannot reach. A clinical depigmentation system works below the surface to interrupt the overproduction cycle.

## What is Melanopro and how does it compare to Cosmelan

Both are professional-grade depigmentation peel systems designed for stubborn pigmentation. Melanopro is delivered through the Dermalogica clinical range and offers a structured treatment-and-aftercare protocol for measurable fading.

## What the depigmentation journey looks like

Clinical pigmentation correction is not a once-off treatment. It typically involves a professional application, a strict home-care protocol, sun protection and follow-up assessments to track fading over several weeks.
