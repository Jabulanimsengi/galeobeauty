## Why purple shampoo only does so much

Purple shampoo is great for at-home maintenance, but it only sits on the outside of the hair. Overusing it can actually dry your hair out and make the ends look muddy. A professional salon toner penetrates the cuticle for a smooth, even, and long-lasting result.

## The power of a Hair Gloss

A toner doesn't just fix colour; it acts as an intensive conditioning treatment. It smooths the highly porous outer layer of bleached hair down, resulting in incredible, glass-like shine that lasts for 4 to 6 weeks.
