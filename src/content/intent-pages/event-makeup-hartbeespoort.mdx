## Why event makeup needs more than everyday products

Special occasion makeup usually needs to hold for longer, photograph more cleanly and still survive heat, dancing, tears or long hours. That changes the way the look should be built from the start.

## Choosing the right level of glam

Some clients want polished softness, while others want more drama and definition. The best result usually depends on the event, what you are wearing and how comfortable you are with a stronger look.

## How to look camera-ready without feeling overdone

Beautiful makeup should still feel balanced up close. The goal is not to hide your features but to make them read better in person and on camera for the full event.
