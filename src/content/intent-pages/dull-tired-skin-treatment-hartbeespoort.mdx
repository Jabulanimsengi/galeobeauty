## Why skin starts looking tired even when you are not

For a lot of people, dull skin is not really about age. It is about buildup, dehydration, stress, travel, weather changes or a routine that is no longer giving the skin what it needs.

That is why the right glow treatment is not always just "more exfoliation." Sometimes the skin needs hydration and barrier support just as much.

## When Hydraderm is the better fit

If your skin feels both congested and dehydrated, [Hydraderm](/prices/dermalogica/hydraderm) is often a strong option because it can help with deep cleansing and hydration in the same treatment.

## When Nanoinfusion or Luminfusion make more sense

[Nanoinfusion](/prices/dermalogica/nanoinfusion) is often considered when the goal is a fresher, more serum-infused finish without moving into full microneedling territory. [Luminfusion](/prices/dermalogica/luminfusion) is a useful option when clients want brightness, smoothness and a more polished event-ready look.

## A smarter way to choose

- If your skin feels rough and built up, start by asking about exfoliation-led treatment.
- If it feels tight, travel-worn or flat, hydration may be the bigger priority.
- If you want one adaptable starting point, [Pro Skin 60](/prices/dermalogica/pro-skin-60) gives more room to personalise the treatment on the day.

## What to expect from the right treatment

The goal is not an artificial "glass skin" promise. It is skin that looks more rested, reflects light better and feels smoother and more comfortable after the treatment than it did before.
