Filing away at a heavy callus for 10 minutes with a pumice stone is exhausting and ultimately ineffective. Our Callus Peel treatment acts like a specialized facial for your feet, chemically lifting the dead skin away painlessly in just 15 minutes.

After we reveal the fresh, soft skin, we need to protect it. Plunging the feet into warm, melted Paraffin wax creates an airtight seal that forces deeply nourishing oils into the skin while providing incredible, soothing heat therapy for aching joints.
