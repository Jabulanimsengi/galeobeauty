## Tape-In Extensions: Fast and Flat

Tape-ins use medical-grade adhesive strips to sandwich your natural hair between two extension pieces. They lie extremely flat against the head and are very fast to install. They are great for adding volume to fine hair, provided you don't wear high ponytails often, as the tapes can peek out.

## The Genius Weave: The Premier Weft

The Genius Weave is an ultra-thin, flexible track of hair. It is installed using a hidden beaded row foundation and sewn securely. Because there is no glue or tape, and the track is so thin, it can be seamlessly hidden even in fine hair, making high ponytails possible.

## Maintenance and Lifting

Both methods require you to return every 6-8 weeks as your natural hair grows out. Tape-ins must be completely soaked off, re-taped, and reapplied. The Genius Weave simply has its beads unlocked and the entire row neatly slid back up to the root, which many clients prefer.
