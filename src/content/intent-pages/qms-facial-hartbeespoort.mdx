## When QMS is usually the better choice

QMS facials tend to suit clients who want their skin to look fresher, fuller and more comfortable rather than aggressively resurfaced. If your skin feels dry, flat, stressed or less bouncy than it used to, this is often a more natural fit than a stronger peel-first approach.

## The QMS options we most often match to client goals

### [Collagen Facial](/prices/qms/collagen-facial)

This is the option many clients choose when the goal is plumper-looking, firmer-feeling skin with a polished finish.

### [Rejuvenating Facial](/prices/qms/rejuvenating-facial)

This works well when the skin looks tired, dull or in need of a visible refresh before an event or important week.

### [Sensitive Skin Facial](/prices/qms/sensitive-skin-facial)

If your skin is easily irritated, feels tight or reacts quickly to stronger actives, this is often the more sensible place to start.

## What results people usually want from a QMS facial

Most clients booking a QMS facial are not looking for the harshest possible treatment. They want skin that feels more hydrated, sits better under makeup, reflects light more evenly and looks less tired overall.

That makes these facials especially useful for:

- dry or depleted skin
- mature skin that needs softness and glow
- pre-event skin preparation
- clients who want visible improvement without a recovery-heavy plan

## When a peel may still be the better option

If your main concern is acne marks, pigmentation or stronger texture correction, a peel such as [Chemical Peel](/prices/qms/chemical-peel) or a Dermalogica corrective treatment may be more relevant than a hydration-led facial.

## Booking advice for Hartbeespoort clients

If you live in Hartbeespoort and want a skin treatment that feels polished, effective and easier to fit around work or events, a QMS facial is often a strong first booking. It gives you a clearer sense of what your skin responds to before committing to something more corrective.
