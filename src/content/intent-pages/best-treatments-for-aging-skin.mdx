## Understanding Best Treatments For Aging Skin

As skin chronologically ages, it undergoes a dramatic biological slowdown. It is not just about wrinkles; aging skin fundamentally behaves differently than it did in your twenties. 

The three primary hallmarks of aging skin are:
1. **Severe Trans-Epidermal Water Loss (TEWL):** The lipid barrier significantly degrades, meaning the skin loses its ability to physically hold onto water. Aging skin naturally becomes chronically dry and paper-thin.
2. **Lethargic Cellular Turnover:** A teenager sheds a layer of dead skin every 21 days. In your fifties, that process can take up to 60 days. The dead skin cells build up, creating a highly textured, muddy, and dull complexion that makes wrinkles look twice as deep as they actually are.
3. **Melanin Misfiring (Age Spots):** Decades of cumulative UV exposure finally surface, causing localized cells to aggressively over-produce pigment, resulting in massive, stubborn brown spots across the cheeks and hands.

## Our Professional Advice

Treating aging skin requires a highly aggressive, active routine at home combined with heavy, resurfacing clinical treatments at the spa. "Gentle, hydrating" routines are no longer enough; you must scientifically force the skin to perform.

### 1. The Retinol Requirement
If there is one solitary ingredient you must use for aging skin, it is a retinoid (Vitamin A). 
- **The Routine:** Retinol is mathematically proven to hack your skin's biological clock. It enters the cell receptor and violently forces the skin to turn over at a youthful speed. It sheds dead skin, physically thickens the thinning dermis by producing new collagen, and effectively irons out fine lines. *Warning:* It must only be used at night, and you must wear heavy SPF the next day.

### 2. Powerful Peptide Integration
While retinol acts as the architect telling the cells to build, peptides act as the literal building blocks of the new collagen.
- **The Routine:** Utilize a dense peptide serum every single morning. These chains of amino acids plunge into the skin and instantly send signals to repair the damaged collagen matrix, resulting in visibly firmer, bouncier skin.

### 3. Professional Cellular Exfoliation
Because aging skin refuses to shed itself, you must remove the dead barrier in the salon so your expensive retinol and peptides can actually penetrate.
- **The Clinical Solution:** Book a [Dermalogica Pro Power Peel](/services/dermalogica/pro-power-peel). This intense clinical acid treatment completely dissolves the sluggish layers of dead skin and aggressively fades the severe brown age spots that make the skin look significantly older.

### 4. Extreme Ceramide Moisturization
You cannot use lightweight lotions on aging skin. The barrier needs heavy reinforcement.
- Switch to incredibly rich, dense recovery creams packed strictly with Ceramides and Squalane to permanently patch the microscopic cracks in the thinning epidermis and lock the water violently inside.
