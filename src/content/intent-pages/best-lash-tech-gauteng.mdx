## The Importance of Isolation

A master tech spends the majority of the appointment meticulously isolating a single, individual natural lash before applying the extension. This ensures the extension can grow and fall out naturally without pulling its neighbors out with it.

## Handmade Volume Fans

We do not use pre-glued, heavy cluster lashes. For Volume sets, our artists manually pick up 3 to 6 microscopic synthetic hairs, fan them out perfectly with tweezers, and wrap them around your natural lash for weightless, fluffy density.

## Retention is Everything

With our advanced application techniques and premium medical-grade adhesives, our clients consistently enjoy 3 to 4 weeks of gorgeous wear before needing a 60-minute fill appointment.
