## It usually starts with something feeling off

Most people do not book hair just because they feel like a general salon visit. It usually starts when something is visibly off: the regrowth line is obvious, the blonde has gone yellow, the blowout no longer lasts, or humidity is winning every day.

## Colour, blowouts and smoother hair in one place

At Galeo Beauty, the easiest way to choose the right appointment is to match it to the problem:

- [Tint Roots](/services/hair/tint-roots) when regrowth is the main frustration
- [Balayage](/services/hair/balayage) when the goal is softer dimension and a brighter finish
- [Blow Long](/services/hair/blow-long) when you want a polished result quickly
- [Keratin](/services/hair/keratin) or [Brazilian Medium](/services/hair/brazilian-medium) when frizz and manageability are the real issue

## When the drive from Pretoria feels worthwhile

The trip usually feels most worth it when you want a better outcome than a rushed convenience appointment. That can mean cleaner colour work, more thoughtful smoothing recommendations, or simply the kind of finish that feels easier to maintain after you leave.

## A simpler way to choose the first booking

If you are still figuring out which direction to take, it helps to book around the highest-priority problem instead of trying to solve everything at once. That usually gives you a better first result and makes the next appointment easier to plan.
