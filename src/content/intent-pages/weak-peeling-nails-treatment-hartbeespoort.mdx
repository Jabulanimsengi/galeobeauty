## Why your gel polish keeps chipping

The most common reason for chipping is a mismatch between the **Flexibility of the Nail** and the **Rigidity of the Product**. If your natural nail is thin, it bends dynamically every time you type or pick something up. A standard, thin gel polish cannot handle that degree of movement and will eventually snap its bond with the nail plate, causing a chip.

To stop the chipping, we must fundamentally change the physics of your nail. We do this by applying a higher-viscosity Builder Gel that physically prevents the natural nail from bending to its break-point. When the nail is stationary, the polish stays attached.

## The power of BIAB and Rubber Base

At Galeo Beauty, we specialize in "Nail Reinforcement" using **Rubber Base** and **BIAB**. These systems are uniquely formulated with a higher concentration of cross-linking polymers. This creates a flexible but hard-wearing "cage" around your natural nail.

Unlike traditional acrylics, which can be harsh and require aggressive filing, BIAB is entirely soakable. This means we can strengthen your nails without ever actually touching your natural nail plate with a coarse file. This "non-invasive" strengthening is the only way to heal a nail that has been thinned by years of poor salon habits.

## The repair journey

Repairing a "weak" nail is a biological biological timeline. It takes approximately **4 to 6 months** for a fingernail to grow from the matrix (under the skin) to the free edge. 

When you start a [Structured Manicure Journey](/services/nails) with us, the first 4 months are about "Protection." We keep the layers of your nail from peeling further and provide artificial strength. Once your natural, healthy nail reaches the tip after 6 months, you will find that even "standard" gel polish will suddenly last for weeks because the foundation is finally structurally sound.
