## When styling starts to feel like a battle

The biggest reason people book keratin is not because they want a dramatic
change. It is because their current routine takes too much time for a result
that still does not last.

## Keratin or a stronger smoothing route

[Keratin Treatment](/prices/hair/keratin) is often the first thing clients ask
about when they want less frizz and more control. Depending on your texture and
how sleek you want the finish, [Brazilian Medium](/prices/hair/brazilian-medium)
or [Brazilian Long](/prices/hair/brazilian-long) may also be worth discussing.

## Why the Johannesburg trip can make sense

This kind of booking usually feels worth the drive when the frustration is
ongoing. If your hair has become hard to manage week after week, a more
considered smoothing appointment often feels more useful than another temporary
blowout.

## The real goal

The best result is usually simple: hair that behaves better, takes less effort
and still feels like your hair, just calmer and easier to live with.
