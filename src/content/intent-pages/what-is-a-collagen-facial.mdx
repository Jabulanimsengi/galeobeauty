## Understanding What Is A Collagen Facial

A Collagen Facial is a high-intensity clinical treatment designed to artificially bypass the skin's rapidly declining biological production of type I and type III collagen. As we age, specifically after the age of 25, our body's fibroblasts (the "factories" that build skin structure) begin to dormant at a rate of roughly 1% per year.

While many retail "collagen creams" exist, they are biologically useless for structural repair because the collagen molecule itself is far too voluminous to physically penetrate the epidermis; it simply sits on the surface of your skin until you wash it off. A true [Collagen Facial](/prices/dermalogica) at Galeo Beauty utilizes high-concentration, medical-grade collagen serums combined with advanced delivery systems like Nanoinfusion or Ultrasound. These technologies physically force the active proteins past the cellular lipid barrier and deep into the dermal matrix.

## Our Professional Advice

To maximize the biological return on a collagen facial, we execute a three-stage structural replenishment.

### 1. The Pro-Collagen Vacuum
We must first clear the road. Before infusing new collagen, we utilize deep chemical exfoliation (Lactic Acid) to remove the "deadwood" layer of desensitized skin cells. This ensures the collagen peptides have a direct, unobstructed pathway to the live tissue.

### 2. Biological Attunement
Collagen needs a "trigger" to work. We combine the collagen infusion with active Vitamin C and Copper Peptides. These act as the biological "keys" that turn on the dormant fibroblasts, telling your body to not just accept the external collagen, but to start manufacturing its own brand-new scaffolding again.

### 3. The Moisture-Lock Seal
Collagen is nothing without hydration. Following the infusion, we apply a heavy Hyaluronic Acid occlusive mask. This traps the new proteins inside your skin, creating an immediate, visible "plumping" effect that can last for weeks, significantly softening fine lines and restoring a youthful bounce to the face.
