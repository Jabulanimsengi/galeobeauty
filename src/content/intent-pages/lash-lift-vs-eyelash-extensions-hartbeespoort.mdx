## Eyelash Extensions (The Dramatic Route)

Eyelash extensions are a high-maintenance structural addition. We bond synthetic silk or mink fibres to your natural lashes using a medical-grade cyanoacrylate adhesive. This allows us to physically override your genetics, creating length, curl, and volume that is biologically impossible for your natural lashes to achieve.

The maintenance is intensive. You must avoid oil-based products, sleep in a way that doesn't crush the delicate fans, and return for a "Fill" every 2 to 3 weeks as your natural lashes shed. If you want the [Dramatic 'Glamour' Look](/prices/lashes-and-brows) that completely eliminates the need for eyeliner, extensions are your only option.

## The Lash Lift & Tint (The Natural Route)

A Lash Lift is an organic enhancement of your existing assets. We use a silicon rod and a lifting solution to chemically reset the keratin bonds of your natural lashes into a permanent upward curl. We then apply a concentrated "Blue-Black" tint to the hair. 

Because the tips of most lashes are naturally blonde or sun-bleached, the tint makes your lashes look significantly longer than they appear now. The result is a "Masterful Mascara" look that lasts 6 to 8 weeks. There is **zero daily maintenance**. You can rub your eyes, go swimming, and use any oil-based makeup remover without fear of ruining the results.

## Which requires more natural lashes

The [Elleebana Lash Lift](/prices/lashes-and-brows) is most effective for clients who have healthy, medium-to-long natural lashes. If your lashes are extremely sparse or short, curling them will not create the "fullness" you desire. In these cases, Volume Extensions are necessary to artificially fill in the gaps and create the illusion of a dense lash line.
