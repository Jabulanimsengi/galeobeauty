## Understanding What Is A Brazilian Blowout

A Brazilian Blowout is one of the most famous, highly requested, and frequently misunderstood salon services in the world. It is universally revered as the ultimate "frizz-eraser," but understanding exactly how it achieves this is crucial for managing your expectations and maintaining the results.

Unlike chemical relaxers—which violently break the internal disulfide bonds of the hair to permanently rip out the curl pattern—a Brazilian Blowout does not mathematically alter the internal structure of your hair. 

Instead, it is a **Customizable Liquid Amino-Acid Sealer**. The liquid formula is painted onto the hair and then meticulously flat-ironed at 450 degrees. The extreme heat forces the amino acids to create a rigid, protective protein layer entirely *around* the outside of the hair shaft. This laminate shield forcefully forces the hair cuticle flat, physically blocking out humidity and creating a breathtaking, glass-like shine.

## Our Professional Advice

A Brazilian Blowout is arguably the most versatile smoothing treatment on the market. Here is what you need to know before booking your life-changing [salon appointment](/prices/hair/brazilian-blowout).

### 1. It is NOT a Permanent Straightener
This is the number one misconception. A Brazilian Blowout will not give you perfectly bone-straight hair if you naturally have tight curls. 
- The treatment primarily targets the cuticle to eliminate **100% of frizz** and drastically reduce styling time. 
- It is highly customizable. A master stylist can flat-iron it heavily to remove wave, or flat iron it gently to allow you to keep your beautiful bouncy curls—just completely free of frizz.

### 2. Immediate Gratification
Unlike traditional keratin treatments that force you to walk around with greasy, flat hair for 72 hours before washing it, the Brazilian Blowout is completely finished in the salon.
- Your stylist will wash the formula out immediately after ironing it. You walk out of the salon with clean, completely finished hair. You can put your hair in a ponytail, sweat at the gym, or wash it again the very next day with zero consequences.

### 3. The 12-Week Lifespan
Because it is a laminate coating rather than an internal chemical breaker, the results are semi-permanent.
- The treatment will last between 10 to 12 weeks. Over time, as you wash your hair, the protein seal slowly dissolves. The beauty of this is that there is absolutely zero "line of demarcation." Your hair will simply gradually revert back to its natural texture, meaning you never have an awkward grow-out phase.

### 4. The Sulfate Rule
If you wash your hair with a cheap, drugstore shampoo containing Sodium Lauryl Sulfate, the harsh detergent will rip the expensive protein seal completely off your hair in less than two weeks. You absolutely must use sulfate-free, Brazilian Blowout-approved aftercare products to preserve the massive financial investment you made in your hair.
