## How a Traditional Facial works

A traditional facial is heavily reliant on the therapist's hands. It involves steam, manual squeezing to extract blackheads (which can be painful and leave the skin red), and the topical painting-on of masks and serums that penetrate via natural absorption.

## How Hydraderm works (The Machine Difference)

Hydraderm uses a patented wand with a spiraled suction tip. Step one uses the vortex suction to literally vacuum blackheads and debris out of the pores effortlessly and painlessly. Step two uses the same wand to forcefully hyper-infuse the empty pores with plumping hyaluronic acid and antioxidants.

## The zero-downtime advantage

Because Hydraderm does not require aggressive manual squeezing, there is almost zero inflammation or redness afterward. You walk out with skin that looks incredibly clean, plump, and glowing—ready for a red carpet event the same day.
