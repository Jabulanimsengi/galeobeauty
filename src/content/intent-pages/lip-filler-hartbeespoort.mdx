## When lip filler can make the biggest difference

Lip filler can be a beautiful choice if you want more definition, a softer cupid's bow, better symmetry or a fuller shape that still feels like you.

## Classic lip filler vs Russian lips

The best style depends on your natural lip shape and the finish you love most. Some people want gentle softness, while others prefer a slightly more lifted and defined result.

## Natural lip filler results

A refined result should suit your features, feel balanced from every angle and leave you looking fresher rather than obviously treated.
