## Why luxury nails are not just about the design

The biggest frustration most clients have is not the colour or the art but the fact that their nails lift, chip or look sloppy after only a few days. A luxury nail service starts with better prep, better products and better technique.

## Sculpted extensions vs tips and overlays

Sculpted nails are shaped individually on your hand for a custom fit. Tips are pre-formed and glued. The difference in longevity, fit and shape quality is significant for clients wanting a premium finish.

## Choosing between gel, acrylic, BIAB and rubber base

Each system suits different nail types and lifestyle needs. Some prioritise natural nail health, while others prioritise length, strength or artistic flexibility. The right one depends on what matters most to you.
