## Understanding How To Strengthen Nails

Brittle, peeling, and hopelessly weak nails are rarely a genetic curse. Your nail plate is biologically constructed from tightly packed layers of a structural protein called Keratin—the exact same protein that forms your hair. 

When a nail becomes so "weak" that you can bend it backward without resistance, or so structurally compromised that it constantly peels off in horizontal layers (delamination), it is suffering from a massive imbalance of two environmental factors: Water and Lipids.

**The Brittle/Peeling Matrix (Too Dry):** When your nails are exposed to intense hand washing and harsh chemicals without any oil replacement, the keratin layers completely dry out. Without lipid lubrication holding them together, the layers separate and violently peel apart like an onion.
**The Soft/Bendy Matrix (Too Wet):** The nail plate is fiercely porous. If your hands are constantly submerged in water, the nails absorb massive amounts of moisture. This violently breaks the protein bonds apart, turning the nail into a soft, mushy, hyper-flexible sponge that instantly categorizes it as "weak."

## Our Professional Advice

You cannot simply paint a "nail hardener" over a severely damaged nail plate and expect it to magically heal. True nail strengthening requires you to dictate the environmental moisture levels and supply structural building blocks.

### 1. The Daily Oil Commandment
The absolute most critical step to strong nails is replacing the intercellular "glue" holding the keratin layers together.
- **The Routine:** You must apply a deeply penetrating oil (specifically pure **Jojoba Oil**) to the entire nail and cuticle bed at least twice a day. The molecular size of Jojoba oil is nearly identical to your body’s natural sebum, allowing it to plunge directly *inside* the nail plate, instantly restoring absolute structural flexibility and completely stopping the nails from violently cracking when bumped.

### 2. Abolish the "Nail Hardener" Trap
Many clients buy extreme chemical "nail hardeners" containing formaldehyde to fix weak nails. 
- These products make the nail plate feel as hard as glass. However, glass is incredibly brittle. The moment you jam your finger against a desk, the unnaturally hard nail will not bend; it will violently shatter and break completely off. You do not want "hard" nails; you want highly flexible, heavily moisturized nails that act like a trampoline when struck.

### 3. Glass Files Only
The mechanical tools you use heavily dictate your nail strength.
- Throw away cheap, gritty emery boards and metal nail clippers. When you use clippers to violently "chop" the nail, or rough emery boards to carelessly saw back and forth across the edge, you cause micro-fractures in the keratin layers that guarantee future splitting. Use only a high-quality **Czech Glass Nail File**, and strictly file your nail in one direction slowly to cleanly seal the outer edge.

### 4. Protect Against Water
Water is the ultimate solvent for keratin.
- If you wash dishes, use harsh bathroom cleaners, or take incredibly long soaking baths, you absolutely must wear rubber gloves to shield your nails from waterlogging.
