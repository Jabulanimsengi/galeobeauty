## Why not all Dermalogica treatments are the same

Dermalogica covers a wide range, from maintenance facials to more corrective professional treatments. That matters because many clients searching for a Dermalogica specialist are not really looking for another relaxing facial. They are looking for clearer help with texture, pigmentation, congestion or skin quality that has stopped responding to lighter treatments.

## The kinds of Dermalogica treatments available at Galeo

Depending on the concern, the plan may involve:

- [Pro Power Peel](/services/dermalogica/pro-power-peel) for resurfacing and correction
- [Pro Microneedling](/services/dermalogica/pro-microneedling) for texture and collagen support
- [Nanoinfusion](/services/dermalogica/nanoinfusion) for targeted infusion and skin refresh
- [Luminfusion](/services/dermalogica/luminfusion) for glow and smoother-looking skin
- [Hydraderm](/services/dermalogica/hydraderm) when hydration and barrier comfort matter
- [Melanopro Peel](/services/dermalogica/melanopro-peel) when pigmentation is the bigger concern

## When this type of clinic makes more sense than a standard facial menu

An advanced Dermalogica centre is a better fit when:

- your concern is no longer just dullness or dryness
- you want measurable progress, not just a temporary glow
- your skin needs a phased plan instead of random treatment hopping
- you want stronger options available when the skin is ready for them

## What better skin planning actually looks like

The real value is not only having more treatments on the menu. It is being able to use the right one at the right time. Some clients need calming and barrier support before they need resurfacing. Others are ready for peel work, while others need collagen-focused correction. Matching the treatment stage properly usually matters more than choosing the fanciest service name.
