## Understanding Waxing Vs Shaving

The debate between waxing and shaving is not merely a matter of convenience; it represents two completely different biological mechanisms of hair removal. 

**Shaving** is a strictly superficial process. A razor blade simply slices the hair shaft exactly at the surface level of the epidermis. Because the thickest part of the hair (the root) remains firmly planted inside the follicle, the hair continues its growth cycle uninterrupted. Within 12 to 24 hours, the blunt, sliced end of the hair breaches the surface again, feeling incredibly coarse and creating rapid "stubble."

**Waxing** is a structural extraction. Hard or soft wax heavily adheres to the hair shaft and forcibly rips the entire hair—including the bulb—completely out of the dermal papilla (the blood supply at the bottom of the follicle). Because the follicle is violently emptied, the body has to regenerate an entirely brand new hair from scratch. This biological regeneration takes weeks, and when the new hair finally emerges, it is a brand-new, fine "baby hair" rather than thick, blunt stubble.

## Our Professional Advice

At Galeo Beauty, we adamantly advocate for professional waxing. Shaving is a temporary illusion of smoothness that causes massive trauma to the skin barrier. Here is why the clinical extraction of waxing is superior.

### 1. The Death of the Follicle
When you shave, the follicle remains perfectly healthy and strong.
- When you undergo consistent [Professional Waxing](/prices/waxing-and-tinting), you are causing repeated, intentional trauma to the dermal papilla. Over time, this repeated trauma literally damages the blood supply to the hair. The follicle becomes permanently weakened, meaning your hair permanently grows back thinner, significantly lighter, and in many patches, completely stops growing altogether.

### 2. Epidermal Exfoliation
Shaving with a multi-blade razor aggressively scrapes off the protective acid mantle of your skin, often resulting in "razor burn" (clinical micro-lacerations and bacterial infection).
- Professional waxing acts as a profound mechanical exfoliant. When the wax is pulled off, it cleanly removes the entire top layer of dead, dull skin cells in a single, sterilized motion. Your skin is left flawlessly smooth, highly polished, and radiant without the daily scraping trauma of a razor blade.

### 3. The Ingrown Hair Myth
Many clients fear waxing because they believe it causes severe ingrown hairs. The reality is exactly the opposite if executed properly.
- A dull razor blade forces the hair back under the skin at an unnatural angle, guaranteeing an ingrown hair. Professional hard wax grips the hair securely without sticking to the skin, pulling it out cleanly in the exact direction of hair growth. Provided you adhere to strict post-care exfoliation, waxing drastically reduces ingrowns compared to daily shaving.
