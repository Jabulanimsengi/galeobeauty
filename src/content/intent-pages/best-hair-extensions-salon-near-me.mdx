## 100% European Remy Hair

Not all hair is created equal. 'Remy' means the hair cuticle is kept intact and facing one direction, drastically reducing tangling and matting. Our premium European hair can be washed, heat-styled, and curled exactly like your own hair.

## The Genius Weave Speciality

We specialize in the Genius Weave—the thinnest, most invisible track of hair on the market. It is sewn into a hidden beaded foundation, meaning absolutely no glue or tape touches your natural hair. It is so flat you can wear a high ponytail completely entirely undetected.

## Custom Colour Blending

The secret to natural-looking extensions is the colour blend. We don't just attach the hair; we often custom-tone or 'root melt' the extensions so they transition invisibly into your natural hair colour.
