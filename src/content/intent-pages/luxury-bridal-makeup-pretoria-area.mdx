## The importance of the Bridal Trial

We never guess on your wedding day. The bridal trial, done 1-2 months before the wedding, gives us time to experiment with the perfect foundation match, lash style, and lip colour so the actual morning is completely stress-free.

## Long-wear, HD-ready products

We use premium brands designed specifically for film and photography. These formulas do not cause 'flashback' (when your face looks ghostly white in photos) and are designed to lock onto the skin through sweat and tears.

## Accommodating the whole Bridal Party

Hartbeespoort is a major wedding destination. We regularly handle large bridal parties of 6 or more. You can either hire our salon exclusively for the morning for a VIP experience, or we can travel to your venue.
