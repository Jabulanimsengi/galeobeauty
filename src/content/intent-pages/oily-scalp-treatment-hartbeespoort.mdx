## The daily washing trap

Washing your hair every day strips the natural protective oils. Your scalp panics and overproduces sebum to compensate. Breaking this cycle requires a professional deep cleanse and transitioning to a gentler, balancing home-care routine.

## Professional scalp exfoliation

Just like your face, your scalp needs exfoliation. Professional scalp treatments use gentle enzymatic or physical exfoliants to dissolve the buildup of sebum, dead skin cells, and dry shampoo that regular shampoo leaves behind.

## Protecting the ends

A common issue with oily roots is dry, brittle ends. We customize your treatment by applying a deep-cleansing mask to the root area while simultaneously applying an intensive hydrating treatment to the mid-lengths and ends.
