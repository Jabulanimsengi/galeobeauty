## Why hair gets frizzy and hard to manage

Frizz can be linked to dryness, porosity, texture, weather and previous chemical stress. Understanding that pattern makes it easier to choose the right smoothing path.

## Keratin vs Brazilian smoothing

Both treatments are chosen for smoother, more manageable hair, but they can differ in softness, intensity and how the finish settles over time.

## What smoother hair should still feel like

The goal is not flat, lifeless hair. It is softer texture, less puffiness, easier styling and a finish that still moves naturally.
