## Why this suits low-effort beauty routines

The biggest appeal of a lash lift is that it works with what you already have.
You still look like yourself, just a little more awake and a little less
dependent on mascara.

## Lift, tint or full extensions

[Lash Lift & Tint](/services/lashes-brows/lash-lift-tint) is usually the best fit
when your natural lashes need more curl and visibility. If you want more
fullness than your own lashes can give, [Classic Lashes](/services/lashes-brows/classic-lashes)
may suit you better. [Brow Tint](/services/lashes-brows/brow-tint) can also help
the whole eye area look a bit more finished without much extra effort.

## Why Sandton clients book this specifically

This appointment tends to make sense for people who want a natural result, easy
maintenance and a treatment that quietly improves the morning routine rather
than adding another one.

## The best result

The best lash lift usually looks subtle enough that people notice you look
fresh, not necessarily that you have had something done.
