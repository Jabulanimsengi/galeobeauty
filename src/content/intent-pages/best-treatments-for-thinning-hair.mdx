## Understanding Best Treatments For Thinning Hair

When hair begins to thin, the underlying biology indicates that your hair follicles are systematically miniaturizing. Instead of producing thick, robust, fully pigmented terminal hairs, the follicle shrinks and begins producing incredibly fine, wispy, transparent "vellus" hairs. Eventually, if left untreated, the follicle completely scars over and dies, resulting in true baldness.

The vast majority of hair thinning—particularly pattern thinning at the crown and temples—is driven by genetics and hormonal cascades, specifically the extreme sensitivity of the follicle to DHT (Dihydrotestosterone), which aggressively strangles the blood supply. 

Treating thinning hair requires an aggressive two-tier strategy: chemically halting the hormonal miniaturization process at the root, and visually manufacturing extreme density along the remaining mid-lengths to hide the scalp while you undergo treatment.

## Our Professional Advice

Do not waste thousands of Rands on "miracle hair growth" gummies or unregulated internet oils. Actual progressive thinning requires clinically proven vascular stimulation and high-density cosmetic interventions.

### 1. The Gold Standard: Clinical Vasodilation
If your follicle is starving, you must artificially blast it with oxygen and blood.
- **Minoxidil (5%):** This is the only FDA-approved topical treatment guaranteed to combat thinning. Applied directly to the scalp every day, it violently vasodilates the shrinking blood vessels, overriding the DHT strangulation and forcing the transparent, resting follicles back into thick, terminal growth. You must use it consistently; if you stop, the new hair will fall out.

### 2. Cellular Rejuvenation (PRP & Microneedling)
If you want to drastically amplify the results of topical clinical treatments, you need cellular shock therapy.
- **Scalp Microneedling:** Using a sterile derma-stamp containing tiny needles, you create micro-injuries on the scalp. The body perceives this as trauma and rushes massive amounts of collagen and stem-cells to the scalp to heal it—which coincidentally supercharges the dying hair follicles.

### 3. Immediate Cosmetic Density
While you wait the 4 to 6 months required for Minoxidil to actually grow new hair, you must manufacture volume immediately. 
- Eliminate heavy creams and extreme heat styling that glue the thin hairs to your scalp. 
- Book an appointment for a strategic [Haircut](/prices/hair/cut-blow-short). A trained stylist will completely restructure your layers to hide the thinning spots and build massive invisible bulk.
- If you have lost too much density, consult our Galeo Beauty specialists for [Hair Extension](/prices/hair/hair-extensions) integration. Using microscopic tape-ins or keratin bonds, we can flawlessly hide advanced thinning without placing tension on your fragile remaining roots.
