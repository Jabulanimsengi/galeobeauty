## The Standard Spa Facial

A traditional spa facial is wonderful for stress relief. It heavily features facial, neck, and shoulder massage, steam, and the generic application of sweet-smelling topical masks. It is a surface-level 'fluff and buff' that makes you feel great for 24 hours.

## The Dermalogica ProSkin 60

This is a bespoke, clinical treatment divided into 10-minute modules. Depending on your Face Mapping, one 10-minute module might involve Lactic Acid exfoliation, while the next utilizes electrical modalities (like high-frequency or Nanoinfusion) to force active Retinol deep into the dermis.

## Do I have to lose the relaxation

Absolutely not. While a ProSkin 60 focuses heavily on clinical extraction and active infusion, it still incorporates Dermalogica's signature touch therapies (targeted massage) while the advanced masques are setting, giving you the best of both worlds.
