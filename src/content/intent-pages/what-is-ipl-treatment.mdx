## Understanding What Is IPL Treatment

Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) is a highly versatile, non-ablative clinical light therapy. The term "non-ablative" means the powerful light energy plunges completely through the top layer of your skin without burning, cutting, or removing the surface layer. 

Instead, the massive burst of high-intensity light violently detonates deeply within the dermis, targeting highly specific biological culprits that cause the skin to appear aged, tired, or chaotic. IPL is uniquely capable of treating three completely different severe aesthetic concerns simultaneously during a single flash:

1. **Vascular Lesions (Redness):** The light specifically violently heats the hemoglobin inside broken capillaries and Rosacea flushing. The thermal shock causes the broken vein walls to instantly collapse, glue together, and be absorbed by the body, eliminating the red "spider web" appearance.
2. **Pigmented Lesions (Brown Spots):** Decades of sun damage cause melanin to clump together in massive freckles and deep age spots. The IPL flash shatters these dense brown pigment clusters into tiny fragments, which the body then naturally pushes to the surface and sheds like a dry scab.
3. **Collagen Stimulation:** The intense heat generated in the dermis "tricks" the skin into believing it has suffered a burn. In a panic, the body triggers a massive rush of fibroblasts to rapidly produce brand-new collagen, resulting in drastically tighter, firmer skin over the following weeks.

## Our Professional Advice

An [IPL Photofacial](/prices/dermalogica) is considered the gold standard for "total color correction" when a client’s face is riddled with chaotic reds and browns.

### 1. The "Pepper" Effect (Post-Treatment Pigmentation)
Clients often panic immediately following an IPL session because their skin initially looks worse.
- **What to Expect:** Within 24 hours of the treatment, every single brown age spot that was successfully hit by the light will aggressively darken, looking exactly like coffee grounds or black pepper sprinkled across your face. This is the biological proof the treatment worked! The pulverized melanin has been pushed to the surface. Do not pick or scrub these spots; they will organically flake off in 7 to 10 days, revealing flawless, clear skin underneath.

### 2. Not a Single-Session Miracle
IPL requires cumulative, compounded thermal trauma.
- You will not achieve 100% flawless skin after one 45-minute session. A single flash can only shatter a specific horizontal layer of pigment or coagulate a specific layer of veins. You typically require a strict series of **3 to 6 sessions**, spaced precisely 4 weeks apart, to completely obliterate deep vascular networks and heavy decades-old sun damage.

### 3. The Melasma Warning
IPL is not a cure-all for every type of pigmentation. It is actually highly contradictory for Melasma.
- Sunspots are caused by UV damage; Melasma is caused by severe internal hormonal misfiring. Because IPL generates massive amounts of aggressive dermal heat, using an IPL machine on hormonally triggered Melasma will actually severely hyper-stimulate the pigment factory, making the Melasma permanently, horrifically darker. Your esthetician must properly diagnose the origin of your spots before flashing.
