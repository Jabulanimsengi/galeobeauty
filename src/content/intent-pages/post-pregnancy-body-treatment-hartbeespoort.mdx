## Targeting the post-baby belly

Often, what remains is an isolated pocket of fat that doesn't respond to the gym. Fat Freezing (Cryolipolysis) can safely target and crystallize these fat cells, allowing your body to naturally eliminate them over a few months.

## Intimate wellness after childbirth

Vaginal laxity and related concerns are incredibly common but rarely talked about. Our non-invasive vaginal tightening treatments use advanced technology to stimulate collagen production and restore tone to the vaginal tissue comfortably and privately.

## Why non-surgical is a great starting point

Surgery requires significant downtime, which is difficult with children. Our treatments are 'in-and-out', meaning you can return to your daily life immediately while your body gradually improves over the coming weeks.
