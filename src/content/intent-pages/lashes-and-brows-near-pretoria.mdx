## Decide how much definition you actually want

The easiest way to choose between lashes and brows services is to think about
the finish, not the terminology. Some people want to look a little more awake.
Others want fuller texture that still feels wearable day to day.

## Extensions, lift or tint

[Classic Lashes](/services/lashes-brows/classic-lashes),
[Hybrid Lashes](/services/lashes-brows/hybrid-lashes) and
[Volume Lashes](/services/lashes-brows/volume-lashes) all change the eye area in
different ways. If you would rather work with your natural lashes, a
[Lash Lift & Tint](/services/lashes-brows/lash-lift-tint) often gives a lighter,
easier result. [Brow Tint](/services/lashes-brows/brow-tint) can pull the whole
look together with very little effort.

## A good option for easier mornings

These bookings usually work best when you want to cut back on daily mascara,
filling in brows, or trying to create definition from scratch every morning.
The goal is not to look overdone. It is to wake up looking a little more
finished already.

## Why Pretoria clients come through

The trip tends to make sense when you want a more thoughtful result and a more
settled appointment. That matters even more with lashes and brows, where small
details make a big difference to how natural or balanced the final look feels.
