## Classic Lashes: The Natural Enhancement

Classic eyelash extensions utilize a strict 1:1 application ratio—one individual synthetic fibre is meticulously bonded to one isolated natural lash. This is a linear enhancement; it does not add density or "fill gaps," it merely extends the length and intensifies the pigment of the lashes you already possess.

Biologically, Classic lashes are ideal for clients with a thick natural lash line. If you have 100 natural lashes per eye, we can apply 100 extensions. However, if you have sparse lashes with large gaps, Classic lashes will actually **emphasize those gaps** by making the existing hairs longer and further apart. For these clients, we must move into the physics of Volume fans.

## Volume Lashes: Density and Fluff

Volume lashes (often termed Russian Volume) represent a sophisticated engineering solution to sparse natural lashes. Rather than applying a single heavy extension, we create a "fan" of 2 to 6 ultra-fine, lightweight synthetic hairs. This fan is then wrapped 360 degrees around your natural lash, creating a "locked" bond that is significantly more robust than the traditional side-bond used in Classics.

The weight of a 3D or 4D Volume fan is chemically calibrated to be lighter than a single thick Classic extension. This means we can create massive, fluffy density without overloading the hair follicle. This is the only way to achieve a "blackout" lash line for clients with genetically thin or damaged lashes.

## Hybrid Lashes: The Best of Both Worlds

Hybrid lashes are a deliberate architectural mix of 50% Classic and 50% Volume fans. This technique breaks the "mannequin" uniformity of a standard set to create a shattered, wispy, and textured appearance. 

This style is particularly effective for mimicking the growth pattern of natural lashes, where some hairs are naturally longer and thicker than others. It provide the darkness of a Volume set but with the defined, pointed tips of a Classic set. At Galeo Beauty, we recommend [Hybrid Lashes](/services/lashes-and-brows) for first-time clients who want more than a mascara look but aren't ready for the extreme density of Mega Volume.
