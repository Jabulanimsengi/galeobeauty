## When your skin needs more than a reset at home

There is a point where changing cleanser, adding a serum or trying another mask
does not really solve the problem. That is usually when a treatment becomes
useful, not only to improve the skin on the day, but to help you work out what
direction makes sense next.

## Facials, peels and more treatment-led options

[Pro Skin 60](/services/dermalogica/pro-skin-60) is a good all-round place to
start when you want a treatment shaped around your skin. If hydration and skin
comfort are the priority, [Hydraderm](/services/dermalogica/hydraderm) often
makes sense. If your concern is more about resurfacing or stronger correction,
[Pro Power Peel](/services/dermalogica/pro-power-peel) or
[Collagen Facial](/services/qms/collagen-facial) may be the better fit.

## Why Pretoria clients come through for skin appointments

The trip usually feels worthwhile when you want a more considered treatment and
enough space to actually think about your skin properly. That matters most when
you are trying to move beyond generic maintenance and choose something with more
purpose.

## A calmer way to choose the next step

The most useful first appointment is often the one that helps you narrow the
real issue. Once you know whether the focus should be hydration, clearing,
brightness or resurfacing, the rest of your skin plan becomes much easier to
build.
