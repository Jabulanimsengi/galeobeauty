## Understanding Dark Circles Treatment

The periorbital area (the skin directly surrounding your eye socket) is biologically unique. It is up to ten times thinner than the skin found anywhere else on your body. Because the tissue lacks deep subcutaneous fat and possesses almost zero sebaceous (oil-producing) glands, it is incredibly fragile and completely transparent.

When you present with dark circles, you are actually viewing two distinct biological phenomena through this transparent window. 

**Vascular Hollows (Blue/Purple):** If you are sleep-deprived, heavily stressed, or genetically prone, the highly fragile capillary network underneath the eye becomes engorged with deoxygenated, slow-moving blood. Because the skin is incredibly thin, this dark, stagnant blood physically shows straight through the surface, creating a severe purple/blue shadow. 

**Pigmented Circles (Brown/Black):** For clients with higher melanin production or severe allergies (who constantly violently rub their eyes), intense friction causes the melanocytes to panic and dump brown pigment directly into the delicate under-eye tissue, staining it permanently brown.

## Our Professional Advice

You cannot treat a hollow, blue vascular circle with the same brightener you use for a brown pigmentation stain. Your esthetician must correctly diagnose the biological cause.

### 1. Infusion over Application (Nanoinfusion)
Heavy eye creams physically cannot penetrate.
- Because the under-eye area lacks oil glands, slathering a massive glob of thick hydration cream simply causes it to sit on the surface, entirely blocking the pores and causing painful Milia (tiny white cysts). We utilize **Nanoinfusion therapy**—using microscopic silicone cones to aggressively press pure Hyaluronic Acid directly past the lipid barrier, perfectly plumping the microscopic crepe lines from underneath.

### 2. The Structural Tear Trough
If the circle is a physical shadow, you require geometry, not skincare.
- Run your finger from the inner corner of your eye down your cheek. If there is a massive, deep physical trench (the tear trough) caused by genetic skull structure, no eye cream in the world will fix the shadow it casts. This requires a precise medical injection of Dermal Filler directly onto the orbital bone to physically push the skin upward to catch the light.

### 3. Lymphatic Depuffing
Stop retaining cellular waste water.
- If you wake up with massive, heavy bags that entirely ruin your makeup application, your lymph nodes under your ear are blocked. Intense manual lymphatic drainage massage manually squeezes the stagnant fluid away from the delicate eye tissue, physically flattening the severe puffiness instantly.
