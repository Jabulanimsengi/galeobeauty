## What is Brow Lamination

Think of it as the ultimate, permanent brow gel. We use a three-step relaxing and setting process to force the hairs to stand up straight. This covers bald patches and creates that highly-coveted, thick, brushed-up editorial look.

## The Importance of the Tint

Many people have 'invisible' fine baby hairs around their brows. When we combine Lamination with a custom-mixed Brow Tint, we dye these invisible hairs, suddenly revealing a much thicker, fuller brow structure you didn't know you had.

## A painless alternative to Microblading

Microblading involves tattooing strokes into your skin, which is painful and highly permanent. Brow lamination utilizes the hair you already have (even if there isn't much), is completely painless, and lasts for a beautifully low-maintenance 6 weeks.
