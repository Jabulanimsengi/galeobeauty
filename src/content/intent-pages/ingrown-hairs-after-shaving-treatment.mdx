## Why waxing is better than shaving

Waxing completely removes the hair root. It takes 3 to 4 weeks for the body to forge a new hair. Over time, the follicle becomes weaker, meaning less hair grows back, and the hair that does return is significantly softer.

## The Ultimate Solution: IPL Hair Reduction

If you want to be completely free of ingrown hairs forever, IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) is the answer. By targeting the melanin in the root, IPL permanently destroys the follicle's ability to produce hair. No hair means no ingrowns.

## Post-care is crucial

We don't just remove the hair; we educate you on post-care. We stock specialized exfoliating scrubs and salicylic acid solutions that gently dissolve dead skin, ensuring fine regrowth never gets trapped.
