## Deep Tissue vs Swedish: Which do you need

Swedish massage is all about relaxation; it uses lighter pressure to boost circulation and calm the nervous system. Deep tissue therapy uses strong, sustained pressure to break up 'knots' (adhesions) in the deeper layers of muscle.

## The Couples Massage Experience

Hartbeespoort is a prime weekend destination. We offer a dedicated, private couples room where you and your partner can enjoy simultaneous massages, making it the perfect start to a romantic weekend away.

## Hot Stone Therapy

For the ultimate winter warmer or deep muscle relaxation, we use heated basalt stones. The heat from the stones penetrates deep into the muscle belly, allowing the therapist to work deeply without causing pain.
