## The Hydraderm Advantage

Instead of just painting a hydrating mask onto the surface of your skin, the Hydraderm machine uses vortex technology to pressure-wash the pores clean and immediately infuse them with plumping serums, pushing hydration deeper than hands ever could.

## Fixing the Barrier

Flooding the skin with water is useless if the barrier is broken (because the water will just evaporate again). We use specialized Dermalogica lipid-rich products to physically patch and repair the outer layer, locking the moisture inside.

## The 'Glass Skin' Event Prep

Because intense hydration instantly plumps out fine lines and creates high light-reflection, this is the absolute best treatment to have 24 hours before a major event or wedding.
