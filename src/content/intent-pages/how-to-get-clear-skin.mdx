## Understanding How To Get Clear Skin

"Clear Skin" is a state of biological equilibrium. It is the absence of active inflammation, excess sebum congestion, and post-inflammatory pigmentation. Getting clear skin is not about "washing more"; in fact, aggressive over-cleansing is the leading cause of chronic breakouts because it destroys the skin's Acid Mantle.

When the Acid Mantle is "shattered", your skin loses its natural antimicrobial defense. Bacteria thrive, and the skin begins to produce massive amounts of oil to desperately repair the damage. To get [Clear Skin](/services/dermalogica), you must stop attacking your face and start rebuilding the biological barrier while systematically clearing existing congestion.

## Our Professional Advice

Achieving high-clarity skin requires a two-week "Detox" followed by a lifetime of chemical regulation.

### 1. The Salicylic Blockade (BHA)
If you have blackheads or clogged pores, you must use an oil-soluble acid. **Salicylic Acid** is the only clinical ingredient that can physically dissolve the "cement" that holds dead skin and oil together inside the pore. Incorporating a 1% to 2% BHA liquid daily ensures that new "plugs" can never form, effectively ending the cycle of breakouts.

### 2. Microbiome Support
Your skin is home to millions of beneficial bacteria. Most acne products kill *everything*, leading to dry, angry, splotchy skin. We recommend a clinical routine centered around **Niacinamide and Prebiotics**. These ingredients feed the "good" bacteria, which then physically crowd out the "bad" *C. acnes* bacteria, leading to a naturally clear and calm complexion.

### 3. Post-Acne Correction
Even after the bumps are gone, the "Clear" look is often ruined by red or dark spots (Erythema). We utilize **ProSkin Peels** to aggressively speed up cellular turnover, forcing the dark, pigmented cells to shed and revealing the flawless, clear skin underneath in half the time it would take naturally.
