## Understanding Best Hairstyles For Frizzy Hair

Frizz is not an aesthetic failure; it is a structural reaction to atmospheric humidity. When the cuticle of your hair is dry and partially lifted, it acts like a sponge, aggressively absorbing moisture from the air. This atmospheric water violently swells the hair shaft, destroying whatever sleek style you originally created and resulting in a messy, chaotic halo of volume.

If you are biologically prone to frizz, spending an hour meticulously flat-ironing your hair bone-straight before stepping into a humid day is an exercise in futility. The moment you sweat or the humidity hits, the hair will revert. 

The core philosophy of styling frizzy hair is **strategic submission**. Rather than fighting the volume and forcing the hair against its natural texture, you must choose styles that *incorporate* the volume, hiding the frizz within the structural architecture of the hairstyle itself.

## Our Professional Advice

When humidity levels peak, you must immediately abandon sleek, straight looks. Here are the professional hairstyles that effortlessly disguise frizz and look infinitely better with extra volume.

### 1. The "Messy" High Bun (The Top Knot)
If you cannot beat the frizz, contain the ends entirely.
- By pulling your hair into a high top-knot, you are hiding the oldest, driest, and most frizz-prone sections of your hair (the tips) firmly inside the bun. The residual frizz around your hairline simply adds to the intentionally "undone," romantic, messy-chic aesthetic.
- *Stylist Hack:* Spray a toothbrush with strong-hold hairspray and gently brush down the immediate hairline flyaways to create a polished perimeter while keeping the bun itself voluminous.

### 2. The Deconstructed Crown Braid
Braids are the ultimate structural armor against humidity because they physically lock the unruly strands into place.
- A loose, deconstructed Dutch or French braid across the crown uses the natural puffy texture of frizzy hair to create incredible, thick 3D volume that slippery, straight hair cannot achieve without massive amounts of texturizing spray. 

### 3. Embrace the Natural Curl (The Wash & Go)
Frizz is just a curl waiting to be defined. 
- Instead of trying to eliminate the volume, heavily drench your soaking wet hair with a thick defining gel. The gel will clump the frizz together into massive, defined ringlets. Once it dries into a hard cast, "scrunch" the hair to break the cast. You now have beautifully defined, voluminous curls instead of chaotic frizz.

If you are exhausted from fighting the weather daily, book a consultation for a [permanent chemical smoothing treatment](/prices/hair), such as a Brazilian Blowout, to permanently sever your hair's reaction to humidity.
