## When you need more than a quick break

Sometimes the body needs more than a pause. Once tension starts sitting in your
shoulders, back or jaw all the time, a proper massage usually feels a lot more
helpful than trying to push through another week.

## Match the massage to what you need

[Swedish Massage (60min)](/services/massages/swedish-massage-60) is a good fit
when the goal is to unwind. [Aromatherapy Massage (60min)](/services/massages/aromatherapy-60)
and [Hot Stone Massage (60min)](/services/massages/hot-stone-60) suit people who
want a more cocooning, restorative feel. [Deep Tissue Massage (60min)](/services/massages/deep-tissue-60)
usually makes more sense when the real issue is knots and stubborn tightness.
