## Understanding How To Prepare For IPL

Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) is a highly aggressive optical intervention. The machine projects blistering flashes of broad-spectrum light that mathematically target dark pigmentation inside your skin.

Because the physics of the machine rely entirely on targeting "darkness," the condition, tone, and chemical state of your skin surface *before* you sit in the treatment chair dictates the entire safety of the procedure. If your skin is not prepared flawlessly, the machine will target the wrong structures. 

If there is fresh melanin generated by a recent tan, extreme surface heat from a hot shower, or chemical photosensitizers remaining on the epidermis, the IPL flash will brutally confuse the surface of your skin for the intended target (a hair bulb or age spot). Instead of passing cleanly through the epidermis, the intense light will violently detonate exactly on the surface, causing permanent hypopigmentation (white scars) or severe thermal burns.

## Our Professional Advice

Preparation for an [IPL Laser Session](/prices/dermalogica) is absolute and non-negotiable. If you arrive at Galeo Beauty having violated any of the following biological protocols, our clinicians will legally refuse to perform the treatment.

### 1. The 4-Week "No Tan" Mandate
This is the most critical rule in all of light therapy.
- The IPL machine targets melanin (brown pigment). If you arrive with a fresh sunburn, a deep sun-tan, or even a fake spray-tan, you have artificially flooded the surface of your skin with dark pigment. 
- The light will instantly smash into the surface pigment and violently burn your entire face or body. **You absolutely must strictly avoid direct sun exposure, tanning beds, and all self-tanning lotions for a full 4 weeks prior to your appointment.**

### 2. Halt All Chemical Exfoliators (Retinol & Acids)
IPL causes deliberate trauma; your skin must be incredibly strong and intact to absorb it.
- **The Protocol:** Exactly 7 days before your IPL session, you must instantly stop using all forms of prescription Retin-A, over-the-counter Retinol, Glycolic Acid, Salicylic Acid, and Benzoyl Peroxide. These powerful ingredients heavily thin the stratum corneum (top layer of the skin). Flashing thin, chemically compromised skin will result in blistering and severe cellular tearing.

### 3. Shave Only (No Plucking or Waxing)
If you are undergoing IPL specifically for Hair Removal, you must leave the root in the follicle.
- IPL destroys hair by targeting the dark bulb of the hair deep inside the follicle. If you wax, tweeze, or use an epilator before your appointment, you physically rip the dark bulb out. With the bulb missing, the IPL flash has nothing to target, rendering the expensive treatment completely useless. **You must cleanly shave the treatment area 24 hours before your session** so the surface is smooth but the root remains implanted.

### 4. Zero Surface Contaminants
You must arrive "bare-faced" or with bare skin.
- Any remnants of thick makeup, heavy body lotions, deodorant, or metallic sunscreens sitting on top of the skin can act as a physical mirror, violently bouncing the light beam back or absorbing the heat and causing the product to literally cook onto your skin.
