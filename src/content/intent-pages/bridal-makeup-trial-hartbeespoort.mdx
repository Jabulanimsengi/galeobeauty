## Why a bridal trial changes the whole wedding morning

A trial removes guesswork. It helps you see how the makeup sits on your skin, how it photographs, and whether the overall look matches the version of yourself you want to remember.

## What to decide during the trial

This is the time to confirm how soft or defined you want the look, what level of coverage feels comfortable, and how the makeup needs to hold up across the ceremony, photos and reception.

## How the final bridal look should feel

The strongest bridal makeup usually looks polished, long-wearing and beautiful in photography while still feeling like you when you look in the mirror up close.
