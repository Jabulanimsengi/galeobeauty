## Understanding How To Prepare For A Lash Appointment

Whether you are receiving a structural Lash Lift (chemical perm) or Eyelash Extensions (cyanoacrylate adhesive bonding), you are undertaking a highly sensitive, microscopically precise cosmetic procedure directly adjacent to your ocular cavity.

In a Lash Extension service, a master artist uses a highly toxic, rapid-curing medical adhesive to glue a single synthetic fiber directly to a single biological hair. This cyanoacrylate adhesive begins curing the absolute millisecond it leaves the bottle, relying heavily on ambient humidity and the exact pH and cleanliness of your natural hair.

If your eyelashes are coated in a microscopic layer of lipid (oil), heavy protein buildup, or stubborn mascara residue, the adhesive physically cannot grasp the keratin of the hair shaft. It will instead glue the expensive extension directly to the oil or the makeup residue. Within 48 hours, as the oil breaks down and the makeup washes away, the entire $150 set of eyelash extensions will catastrophically slide right off your face.

## Our Professional Advice

If you want your [Professional Lashes](/services/lashes-and-brows) to survive a flawless three weeks rather than three days, you must follow the Galeo Beauty isolation protocol exactly 24 hours before you lay down on the table.

### 1. The Total Eradication of Makeup
You must arrive at the clinic with eyes that have been flawlessly cleaned.
- Do not rely on the technician to wash off your heavy waterproof mascara during the appointment. The heavy oil-based solvents required to remove stubborn mascara leave a severe, invisible greasy film over the eyelashes that completely deflects cyanoacrylate glue. 
- You must carefully strip your eyelashes clean the night before using an oil-free micellar water, and arrive the next day completely bare-faced.

### 2. Halt All Caffeine Consumption
You are required to lay completely motionless for two continuous hours.
- If you arrive at the salon having just consumed a massive iced coffee or an energy drink, the intense stimulants will cause your eyelids to violently flutter and twitch involuntarily. A technician physically cannot use heavy, sharp metal isolation tweezers adjacent to your eyeball if your eyelids are rapidly twitching.

### 3. Avoid The Gym Beforehand
Temperature and humidity violently dictate the speed at which the adhesive cures.
- If you engage in heavy cardio directly before the appointment, your core body temperature spikes and your skin heavily secretes salt-infused sweat. The heat radiating off your face will violently accelerate the glue, causing it to "shock cure" (turn brittle and white) before it can properly structurally wrap around the natural lash. Your extensions will snap off within days.

### 4. Remove Contact Lenses
You must bring a lens case and your glasses.
- The highly volatile fumes emitted by the cyanoacrylate curing process will easily become trapped underneath soft contact lenses. This causes intense, highly uncomfortable burning and extreme redness throughout the entire two-hour service. Strip your eyes of all foreign objects.
