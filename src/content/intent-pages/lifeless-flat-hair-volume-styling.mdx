## The 90s Supermodel Blowout

The biggest trend in hair right now is big, bouncy volume. We use specialized round-brushing and velcro-roller setting techniques to bake the volume in as the hair cools, creating massive, soft bounce that lasts for days.

## The right haircut changes everything

If your hair is all one length, the sheer weight will continuously pull it flat. We specialize in cutting 'ghost layers'—internal texturizing that removes weight from inside the haircut, allowing the hair to spring upwards.

## Rethink your washing routine

Most volume problems start in the shower. If you are using a heavy hydrating shampoo on fine hair, it will pull it flat. We will prescribe a lightweight, clarifying routine that deep-cleans the root and adds grit.
