## Why a full bridal beauty plan starts months before the wedding

The best bridal results come from planning: starting skin prep early, booking a makeup trial, confirming hair styling, and scheduling the final nails, lashes and tan close to the day. A last-minute scramble rarely gives the best outcome.

## Everything you can coordinate through one salon

Galeo offers bridal makeup and trials, hair styling and upstyles, nail art, lash extensions, spray tanning, and a full Dermalogica pre-wedding skin programme — all from one booking system with one team.

## How the wedding morning should feel

The goal is a calm, prepared morning where the work has already been done. Skin is glowing, nails are set, lashes are in, and the hair and makeup artist arrives knowing exactly what you want because you already tested it at the trial.
