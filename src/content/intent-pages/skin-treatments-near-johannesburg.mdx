## When products at home stop doing enough

That is usually the moment a treatment becomes useful. Not because your routine
is wrong, but because the skin needs a little more targeted help than products
alone are giving.

## Facials, peels and stronger support

[Pro Skin 60](/prices/dermalogica/pro-skin-60) is often the best all-round
starting point. [Hydraderm](/prices/dermalogica/hydraderm) suits clients who
need hydration and a fresher reset. [Pro Power Peel](/prices/dermalogica/pro-power-peel)
and [Collagen Facial](/prices/qms/collagen-facial) tend to matter more when
the goal is resurfacing, tone or firmer support.

## Why Johannesburg clients make the trip

Usually because they want more guidance and more calm around the appointment.
That matters when you are trying to do something more useful for your skin than
just book another generic facial.
