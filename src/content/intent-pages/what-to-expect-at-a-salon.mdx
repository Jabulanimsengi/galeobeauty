## Understanding What To Expect At A Salon

Walking into a premium clinical aesthetic center or high-end salon is not a monolithic experience. True professional beauty establishments do not simply "provide a service"; they engage in an intense, analytical, and highly structured biological intervention.

Many clients mistakenly believe that a salon is merely a transactional space—you arrive, point at a picture of a haircut or heavy chemical peel, assume the technician will wordlessly execute it, and leave thirty minutes later. This assumption leads to catastrophic miscommunications and severe physiological damage. 

A premium salon operates much closer to a minor medical clinic. Every single treatment—from aggressively altering the pH of your hair during a chemical dye, to burning off layers of the stratum corneum during a peel, to surgically extracting a localized ingrown hair—requires strict protocols, aggressive safety boundaries, and deep psychological alignment between the client and the technician. 

## Our Professional Advice

If you have never visited Galeo Beauty, or any high-end [Professional Aesthetic Salon](/about), you must mentally prepare for a highly consultative, regimented procedure rather than a casual drop-in. 

### 1. The Non-Negotiable Intake Consultation
You cannot bypass the cross-examination.
- Before a professional even touches your skin or hair, you will be heavily audited. We need to know exactly what prescription medications you are on (especially Accutane or heavy antibiotics, which violently thin the skin). We need to know your exact home-care regimen. If you lie about your recent use of Retinol, our chemical peel will literally burn your face. Complete transparency is legally required.

### 2. The Right to Refusal
An elite professional is not a machine that just outputs a requested service. They are the absolute authority on the structural geometry of your body.
- If your hair is severely chemically compromised (snapping like wet spaghetti), the master stylist will mathematically refuse to bleach it platinum blonde. If your natural eyelashes are critically thin and damaged, the lash technician will aggressively refuse to apply massive, heavy Russian Volume extensions. A true professional will always fiercely protect the biological integrity of your tissue over your temporary aesthetic desire.

### 3. The Price of Honesty (The "Fix")
You must speak up during the process, not a week later.
- If the massage pressure is resulting in violent pain rather than relief, or the water temperature is scalding, you must immediately inform the therapist. If you hate the shape of the nail before the acrylic sets, you must stop the technician. We physically cannot correct a structural failure once the chemical has completely cured or the hair has been heavily sheared.

### 4. Post-Care is a Command, Not a Suggestion
When you leave the salon, the treatment is only 50% complete. 
- You will be handed a strict home-care protocol. If you spend $200 on a heavy keratin hair treatment and immediately go home and wash it with a cheap, heavily sulfated drugstore shampoo, you will violently strip the keratin out in one day. Ensure you understand exactly what to avoid before you leave the building.
