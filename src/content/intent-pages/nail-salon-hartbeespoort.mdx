## Manicures that hold up better on weak nails

If your natural nails bend, peel or split easily, a standard gel polish often is not enough on its own. It may still look good at first, but without extra support it can chip or lift faster than you would like.

At Galeo Beauty, **Rubber Base** and **BIAB (Builder In A Bottle)** are often the better fit for clients who want stronger, longer-lasting natural nails without committing straight to acrylic.

## Choosing between gel and acrylic

Choosing the right system usually comes down to the look you want and how your hands are used day to day.

- **Acrylics** make the most sense when you want more length and stronger structure.
- **Structured gels** are often a better fit when you want a cleaner, more natural feel with flexibility for daily wear.

## Pedicures that feel as good as they look

A [Professional Pedicure](/prices/nails) at Galeo Beauty is about more than adding colour. It is a chance to tidy, soften and properly look after feet that may have been ignored for too long.

The focus is on comfort, neatness and a more polished finish, especially if rough heels or tired-looking feet are what brought you in in the first place.
