## Manicures that last on weak nails

If you have naturally "weak" nails that bend like paper or split at the slightest impact, a standard gel polish is a structural mistake. Standard gel has zero internal strength; it is merely a flexible color coat. On a weak nail, the nail will bend, but the polish will not—leading to immediate delamination and chipping.

At Galeo Beauty, we utilize **Rubber Base** and **BIAB (Builder In A Bottle)** systems. These are high-viscosity structural gels that physically thicken the nail plate by up to 200%. This creates a rigid, supportive chassis that allows your natural nail to grow long and strong underneath without the need for aggressive acrylics.

## Choosing between gel and acrylic

Choosing the right system is a matter of biological physics. 
- **Acrylics** provide maximum rigidity and are the only safe way to manufacture extreme length (tips). If you want 2-inch stilettos, acrylics are the only material with the structural integrity to hold that weight. 
- **Gels** (specifically structured gels) provide flexibility. If you work on a keyboard or have a lifestyle where your hands are constantly active, the flexibility of gel prevents the "shatter" effect often seen with acrylics. We assess your daily mechanical impact before recommending a system.

## Pedicures that feel as good as they look

A [Professional Pedicure](/prices/nails) at Galeo Beauty is not just an aesthetic color application; it is a clinical intervention for the health of your feet. We focus on the removal of **Hyperkeratosis** (thickened, dead skin on the heels) using a specialized alkaline callus-remover. 

Unlike aggressive "cheese-grater" files used in low-end salons—which can cause the body to produce even *more* skin in trauma—our approach is gentle and focused on re-balancing the lipid levels of the feet. We finish every pedicure with a deep lymphatic massage to reduce swelling and increase blood flow to the extremities.
