## The Back Facial (Bacial) Explained

You lie comfortably face-down in our tranquil treatment room while the therapist treats your back to a full clinical facial. We steam the skin to soften the heavy body sebum, allowing us to painlessly extract blackheads you couldn't reach.

## Cutting through the oil with Peels

The skin on the back is much thicker and tougher than the face, meaning it can tolerate stronger chemical exfoliants. We often use targeted Pro Power Peels (like Salicylic Acid) to dive deep into the body pores and kill the acne-causing bacteria instantly.

## Fading Body Scars

Body acne tends to leave stubborn, dark post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation marks. Our professional resurfacing treatments accelerate cellular turnover, rapidly fading these dark spots so your back looks smooth and even-toned.
