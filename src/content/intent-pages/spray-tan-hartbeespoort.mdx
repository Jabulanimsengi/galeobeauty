## Why event tanning feels high stakes

When a wedding, holiday or photoshoot is involved, most clients want reassurance that the tan will look even, natural and flattering in every kind of lighting.

## Choosing between spray tan and sunbed

The right option depends on timing, how quickly you need colour, and whether you want an instant cosmetic finish or a more gradual tanning route.

## What a good tan should look like

The best glow usually looks warm and healthy rather than obviously bronzed. It should make your skin look more alive without looking patchy, muddy or unnatural up close.
