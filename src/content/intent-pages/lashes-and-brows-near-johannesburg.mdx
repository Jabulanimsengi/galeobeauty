## Decide what you want the eye area to do

The easiest way to choose is to think about the finish. Some people want a
subtle everyday lift, others want more texture and fullness, and some simply
want brows that need less work.

## Extensions, lift or tint

[Classic Lashes](/services/lashes-brows/classic-lashes),
[Hybrid Lashes](/services/lashes-brows/hybrid-lashes) and
[Volume Lashes](/services/lashes-brows/volume-lashes) all create different levels
of fullness. [Lash Lift & Tint](/services/lashes-brows/lash-lift-tint) is often a
better fit when you want a lighter-maintenance result. [Brow Tint](/services/lashes-brows/brow-tint)
helps pull the whole look together with very little effort.

## Why Johannesburg clients book this

Usually because they want something that improves the daily routine quietly. The
best result feels like you, just a little more defined and much easier to get
ready.
