## Understanding Best Treatments For Damaged Hair

When dealing with hair damage, it is critical to understand that the internet is flooded with DIY remedies (like mayonnaise or avocado masks) that are biologically incapable of fixing the problem. The molecules in food are simply too large to penetrate the microscopic hair cuticle; they just sit on top of the hair making it greasy.

Real, profound damage—such as chemical breakage, extreme heat frying, or severe thinning—involves the structural destruction of the internal Keratin protein inside your hair shaft. 

To actually treat this damage, you require professional treatments formulated with nano-technology. These treatments utilize micro-molecules that plunge deep into the cortex (the core) of the hair to either inject massive hydration, temporarily repair broken disulfide bonds, or provide rigorous external scaffolding to hold the fragile hair together.

## Our Professional Advice

Navigating the salon menu can be overwhelming when your hair is breaking. Here is how Galeo Beauty professionals prescribe the best clinical treatments based on the specific type of damage your hair is facing.

### 1. For Malleable, Gummy, Bleach-Damaged Hair
If you bleached your hair and it now feels slimy or stretches like a rubber-band when wet, your disulfide bonds are shattered. Traditional moisture will actually make it worse by making it *too* soft.
- **The Best Treatment:** A strict clinical Bond-Building Treatment (like Olaplex or K18) applied during your salon visit. This treatment cross-links the broken bonds, immediately restoring rigid strength to the gummy hair so it stops snapping.

### 2. For Coarse, Frizzy, Heat-Fried Hair
If you have flat-ironed your hair every day and it now feels like dry straw, looks dull, and explodes into frizz, you suffer from massive cuticle lifting and massive internal dehydration.
- **The Best Treatment:** A professional [Hair Botox](/services/hair/botox) session. Hair Botox is an intensive, hyper-concentrated deep conditioning mask infused with caviar oil, collagen, and vitamins. It plumps up the dehydrated hair from the inside out and seals the damaged cuticle tightly shut.

### 3. For Unmanageable, Breaking, Chemically Relaxed Hair
If you have thick, chaotic hair that is breaking off due to severe chemical perming or relaxing, you need heavy-duty structural scaffolding.
- **The Best Treatment:** A professional [Keratin Smoothing Treatment](/services/hair/keratin). Rather than breaking the hair down further, liquid keratin is fused into the structural "potholes" of your hair using high heat. It acts exactly like a rigid, protective cast, giving the hair massive temporary strength, eliminating 95% of frizz, and giving you an ultra-glossy finish that lasts up to 4 months.
