## Nails that stay looking finished for longer

The biggest goal is usually simple: not having to think about them again too
soon. A good appointment should make hands and feet feel sorted, not just good
for one day.

## The right booking depends on how you wear them

[Gel Hands](/prices/nails/gel-hands) and [Gel Toes](/prices/nails/gel-toes)
often suit clients who want longer wear. A fuller reset may need something more
complete like [Mani Pedi](/prices/nails/mani-pedi) or
[Pedicure](/prices/nails/pedicure) if the goal is proper maintenance rather
than polish alone.

## Why Johannesburg clients come through

This kind of booking feels worth the drive when you want a better-maintained
result and a calmer appointment than a rushed in-and-out city stop.
