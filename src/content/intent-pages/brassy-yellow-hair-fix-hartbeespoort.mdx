## Why purple shampoo isn't working

Purple shampoo is a maintenance tool, not a fix. If your hair is deeply orange or yellow, purple shampoo can either do nothing, or worse, grab onto the lighter pieces of hair and leave them looking grey or purple while the brassy pieces remain orange.

## The power of a professional gloss

A professional salon toner or gloss doesn't just deposit the correct neutralizing colour; it also has an acidic pH that closes down the hair cuticle. This smooths the hair shaft, resulting in a high-gloss, reflective shine that lasts for weeks.

## Maintenance between full colour appointments

You shouldn't wait until your next full highlight appointment to fix brassy hair. A quick 30-minute toning appointment every 4-6 weeks will keep your colour looking fresh and cool, saving your hair from unnecessary bleach.
