## What makes a facial feel better matched to the client

The best facial is not always the fanciest one. It is usually the one that matches the reason you booked:

- if your skin feels dry, dull or tired, you may want hydration and glow
- if your skin is congested or reactive, you may need a more targeted reset
- if your concern is starting to become texture, marks or stronger correction, the facial may need to lead into a broader skin plan

The trip usually makes the most sense when you want to turn the appointment into more than a quick stop. That might mean a calmer self-care day, a pre-event treatment, or simply a more thoughtful skin appointment than what you have been getting in busier city settings.
